Lokanath Swami has contributed significantly to youth preaching in ISKCON by establishing the Bhaktivedanta Academy for Spiritual Sciences (BASS) in Noida, which aims to impart the teachings of Srila Prabhupada and preserve Vedic culture and wisdom. He also took over the World Holy Name Project in 2006, which increases events and activities for spreading the holy name of Lord Krishna. His inspiring lectures and kirtanas have impacted and motivated countless young people to devote themselves to Bhakti-yoga.
